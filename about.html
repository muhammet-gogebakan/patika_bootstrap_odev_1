OUR STORY
Patika is the product of friendships going back to 2007 and a team dedicated to solving problems in education. Our founding team has led some of the most influential tech education nonprofits in the US and Turkey before starting Patika. In March 2021, we set up Patika to do the obvious: Human capital is the most important resource for companies, so we need to help them create their own human capital - when providing great job opportunities for everyone.
OUR MISSION
Imagine a train-then-hire platform connected to hundreds of companies around the world. Moving talented people from training straight to jobs.Patika is this platform that millions of youth and companies have been imagining for years, but schools failed to deliver. We are here to change it.
